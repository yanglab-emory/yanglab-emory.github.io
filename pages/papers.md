### Selected Publications and Preprints

* **Sini Nagpal**, **Xiaoran Meng**, Michael P. Epstein, Lam C. Tsoi, Matthew Patrick, Greg Gibson, Phillip De Jager, David A. Bennett, Aliza P. Wingo, Thomas S. Wingo, **Jingjing Yang**. (2018).
